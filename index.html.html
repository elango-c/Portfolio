Projects

Commercial Solar Dryer with Solar Tracking Mechanism

As the team lead, I spearheaded the development of a cutting-edge Commercial Solar Dryer. Our project utilized a solar tracking mechanism to optimize solar energy capture, enhancing the efficiency of the drying process. This innovation not only showcased our technical expertise but also demonstrated our commitment to sustainable solutions in agricultural practices.

AGV Kit for Educational and Material Handling Purpose

In our final year, we embarked on the design and implementation of an Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) Kit tailored for educational and material handling purposes. As part of the project team, I oversaw the development of a versatile AGV system capable of navigating complex environments and performing various tasks autonomously. This project not only honed our engineering skills but also addressed real-world challenges in automation and logistics.
